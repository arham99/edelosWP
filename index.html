Hello & Welcome
I'm Arham Zakki Edelo
Geophysical Engineering Graduate
Hello World!, welcome to the virtual version of me. I recently graduated with a B.Eng degree in major of Geophysist, my area specialization includes earthquake/microearthquake(MEQ) processing, seismic data quantitative interpretation, python programming for data science (geophysics), and mapping & geographic information system analyst.
In nutshell, i really love science, technology and art, of course, technology as art itself. I love watching movies and then reviewing their stories and graphical features in my personal blog, designing web, creating video content, and editing photos even though technically it's not my field of education, but yes i just love doing it. I realize earth science and engineering is a tough work field, it takes hard works as a team, integrity, and high responsibility. Therefore, i extremely interested in developing myself to always be ready and fit to compete in this industry.
That's all a brief description of me. I am open to every opportunity that you might offer and so we can work together. My belief is that we only live for this moment, so live this life, do your best, and make yourself happy, cheerio !!!:)
